Chicken and Waffle Bites

Description

You only need 3 ingredients to get the full chicken and waffle experience with these shortcut chicken and waffle bites. Lightly breaded chicken tenders are dipped in pancake batter, and popped into a waffle iron until they are crisp and golden. Serve with syrup for dipping.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound frozen chicken tenders
  • 1 cup pancake mix
  • 1 cup water
  • Vegetable oil spray
  • Syrup for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat a Belgian or regular waffle iron according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  2. Cook the chicken tenders according to package directions.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together pancake mix and water until smooth.
  4. Spray the preheated waffle iron with vegetable oil spray.
  5. Dip each cooked chicken tender into the pancake batter, allowing excess batter to drip off.
  6. Place the battered chicken tender in the center of the waffle iron and close the lid. Cook until golden brown and crisp, about 3-5 minutes.
  7. Remove from the waffle iron and repeat with remaining chicken tenders.
  8. Serve immediately with syrup for dipping.
